My Favourite Books!

My 3 favourite books as of 2024

The Lord of the Rings


This is the best fantasy book ever written. It simply has not been matched.

Das Kapital


Whilst there has been many strides in critique of the political economy since then, the leaps forward made by Marx in Das Kapital revolutionised the ability of the world to analyse the capitalist mode of production.

The Way of Kings

Whilst I'm not entirely sold on the direction of the series as a whole, as a first book in a fantasy series, the way of Kings really hits it out of the park.
